About Me

I'm a full stack web developer and recent General Assembly graduate. I have a strong passion for coding, design, problem solving and learning new technologies. I love how powerful code can be; from single page apps to a massive Rails stack, the possibilities are limitless.

My experience includes many areas of web development from writing Ruby and JavaScript in frameworks such as Rails and AngularJS, to designing web pages and enhancing UX with HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery. I also like to stay current, teaching myself new skills such as Apple's Swift.

Apart from programming, I enjoy cycling, traveling and exploring new cities. You can also find me regularly completing courses on Udemy. To relax, I try to catch as many films as possible. If you want to learn more about me please reach out via one of the contact links below.
